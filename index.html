FAQs:

Q: Are there clearer pictures of the "After" available?

A: Not for free, it appears - These pictures are provided by Digital Globe's Open Data project. Better pictures are available for a fee.

Q: What are the smeary diagonal lines in some areas?

A: These area coincide with hillsides, and appear to be where the image was stretched to fit over the topography. The imagery was taken from an angle (the satellite was not straight overhead).
